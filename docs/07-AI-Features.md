# Inzane's Food AI Features
Version: 1.0

---

# Overview

This document defines the AI-powered features and automation capabilities supported by the Inzane's Food platform.

The website should be designed with future AI integration in mind.

Some features may be displayed as "Coming Soon" or "Future Ready" until implemented.

The design should make it easy to integrate OpenAI, n8n, Supabase, GoHighLevel, WhatsApp, Messenger, or future AI providers.

# Smart Upselling

Purpose

Increase average order value.

Examples

Suggest Classic Milk Tea

Suggest Almond Jelly

Customer orders Asado Siopao

Suggest Small Milk Tea

Customer orders Yang Chow

Suggest Lumpiang Shanghai

Rules

Recommend no more than two additional items.

Suggestions should feel helpful, not aggressive.
